Welcome to BeamCAD Lite

The goal of this stage is to provide an intuitive capacity checking software that shows a diagram and a series of calculation.

The aim is to produce something that works and teaches something in a "new" way. Looking to solve the capacity of a given beam and explain the background concepts as intuitively/graphically as possible.
