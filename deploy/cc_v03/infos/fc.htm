$$$f'_c$$$ - Concrete Strength

Characteristic compressive strength at 28 days after concrete pour.

AS3600 1.1.2 limits $$$f'_c$$$ to between 20MPa and 100MPa.
3.1.1.1 lists standard grades: [20, 25, 32, 40, 50, 65, 80, 100]MPa
Determined by standard grade/curing conditions (AS1379) or Determined statistically from tests (AS1012.9)
